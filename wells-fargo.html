Wells Fargo

I was one of the main designers on the latest product from Wells Fargo Enterprise called Vantage.
Product Design
User Experience
UX Research

Wells Fargo's Vantage is a new digital banking platform that was designed to provide customers with a more personalized and intuitive banking experience. The platform was launched in 2022 and has been well-received by customers, with many praising its ease of use and its wide range of features. I was a Senior Lead Product Design contractor on the Vantage team and was responsible for the design of the platform's core features. My work included working closely with project managers, design managers, stakeholders, and the research team to understand the business goals and needs of our enterprise clients. I also had to anticipate what needed to be designed and execute pixel-perfect designs, utilizing our current design system while also adding new components and variants in our tool of choice, Figma.

My Role

Work closely with project managers, design managers, stakeholders, and the research team to understand the business goals and needs of our enterprise clients. Anticipate what needs to be designed and execute pixel-perfect designs. Utilize our current design system while also adding new components and variants in our tool of choice, Figma. Collaborate with developers to implement my designs. Conduct user research and usability testing to ensure that my designs are meeting the needs of our users. Present my designs to stakeholders and get feedback. Iterate on my designs based on feedback. Work with the team to launch the platform on time and within budget.

Challenges

One of the biggest challenges I faced was working with a large and complex team. There were many different stakeholders with different needs and priorities, and it was important to be able to balance all of these needs while still delivering a high-quality product. Another challenge was working with a tight deadline. The platform had to be launched on time and within budget, so there was no room for error.

Solutions

I addressed these challenges by being organized and efficient. I created a detailed project plan and communicated regularly with the team to ensure that we were on track. I also made sure to get feedback from stakeholders early and often so that I could make changes to my designs as needed.

Results

The Vantage platform was a success. It was launched on time and within budget, and it has been well-received by customers. The platform has also been successful in terms of business metrics, with Wells Fargo reporting that it has seen a significant increase in customer engagement since the launch of Vantage.

Conclusion

I am proud of the work that I contributed to the Vantage project. The platform has been a success and has helped Wells Fargo to improve its customer experience. I am confident that the platform will continue to be a valuable asset to Wells Fargo for many years to come.

Key Takeaways

Be organized and efficient. Communicate regularly with the team. Get feedback from stakeholders early and often. Be flexible and adaptable. Celebrate your successes and learn from your failures.
